A Bit About Me

Data analyst with 10+ years in finance, helping teams turn operational data into clear, practical decisions.

I’m currently completing my MSc in Data Analytics for Business at KEDGE Business School in Bordeaux. Before moving into analytics, I spent over 10 years in finance operations and project accounting in the construction industry. That experience taught me how much a single reliable data point can change a budget discussion, a forecast, or a project review.

Most recently, I worked as a Business Intelligence Analyst intern at Amplitude Laser Group, a leading manufacturer of femtosecond lasers. I supported their after-sales team by building dashboards, cleaning and validating data, and highlighting the metrics that matter most for day-to-day decisions.
